WELCOME TO WAY 4 AFRICA

Karibuni!

Welcome to the new WAY website and a new WAY to engage in healthy, respectful, mutually rewarding cross-cultural relationships. While you are here we hope you discover new ways to engage with people and groups from cultures and countries that differ from your own.

WAY is a small educational non-profit organization. Since 2004, WAY has sought to strengthen bridges of understanding between Americans and East Africans as well as between diverse groups of people in the USA.

That idea became a reality in 2004 when WAY officially became a 501 (c) (3) non-profit organization. Since that time Sylvia has served as advisor and/or tour organizer for individuals, families, church groups and non-profit organizations traveling to the continent.

We welcome the opportunity to serve each and every one of you.

AVAILABLE SERVICES

-for individuals, churches, schools, colleges and other organizations

  • Consulting, Speaking, Teaching, Preaching
  • Assisting with preparation for overseas travel
  • Facilitating cross cultural activities and events
  • Providing orientations for international students, visitors and guest, including tours to acquaint them with urban and suburban America
  • Organizing and leading learning/study journeys to East Africa for Americans of all ages
