THE ARTIST.

Sincere Rhythm & Blues recording artist's tend to bring a sense of sensuality and urgency to their audiences, and Daniel SkyHigh McClain, a Boston, Ma. currently residing in Atlanta, Ga., is determined to do so by taking the music industry by storm in contributing his unique style to the smooth genre that is R&B/Soul. He's earned himself a spotlight as he's written for and sang with respected pioneers such as The Legendary Earth Wind & Fire, recording artist Blu Cantrell, music mogul Ronnie Garrett, and Mr. Tyler Perry. With the release of his single “Your Love Is”, written by him and produced by Deshawn "Big D" Hendrickson (Keyshia Cole, Blu Cantrell), Daniel SkyHigh McClain is anticipating a moment of his own to share with the world what music really means to him.
