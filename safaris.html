WAY Safaris

A WAY Learning/Service Safari is not your typical safari or mission trip. WAY focuses on educating Americans, empowering Africans and establishing cross- cultural relationships that will last a lifetime. WAY Learning/Service Safaris are also more affordable than luxury tours and volunteer opportunities are arranged according to the interest and needs of group members.

On a WAY Safari you will live simply but comfortably in safe, secure guesthouses, conference centers, hotels or homes rather than five star hotels. You will also have more flexibility, more opportunities for group interaction, more time for in-depth discussions and more personal attention as part of a small group of learners (5-20 people) rather than a large herd of tourist.

On a WAY Safari you will listen to and learn from the true experts on Africa, namely Africans, as well as, African Americans and others who have spent extended periods of time on the continent. As Africans discus their joys and challenges you will gleen about their culture, history, economics and politics.

Our staff of experienced American and Kenyan professionals represents a wide body of expertise guaranteeing hassle-free experience that includes step-by-step assistance with pre-trip planning pertaining to immunizations, visas, packing, safety precautions, familiarity with cultural norms, social etiquette, etc.
