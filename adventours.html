LOCAL/REGIONAL ADVENTOURS

WAY Adventours introduce Visitors and International Students to Atlanta and the Southeast. Knowledgeable guides provide personal insights as well as standard information about the places visited. Adventours are normally arranged for 1 – 25 people but larger groups can be accommodated with sufficient notice.

CITY TOURS

Provide insight into American culture, history and form of government by seeing traditional tourist sites and lesser known places of interest in and around downtown Atlanta

REAL ATLANTA TOURS

Narrated 4 – 6 hour excursions through urban Atlanta highlighting African American history, culture and accomplishments. A “must” for those wanting the backstory of the work of Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr.

HISTORY, HIGHLIGHTS, AND INSIGHTS

Small groups study African American communities, culture and the struggle for Civil Rights by spending 2 – 3 days exploring Americus/Albany and coastal Georgia, Selma/Montgomery/Birmingham Alabama, sea island Gullah societies and/or other places in the Southeast.

DIY-DESIGN IT YOURSELF

Local and international individuals, institutions, corporations, civic or religious organizations create their own unique excursions that are consistent with their mission, objectives and interests.
